each directory stores 12 trials with the number of zebrafish displayed in the name of the directory (1 AB is for 12 trials of 1 zebrafish AB)
for the directory 20AB, the data show only the positions of the individuals without their identities. The first column is for the time, then columns go by two (for x position then y position)
For the directories 2AB, 3AB, 5AB, 7AB and 10AB, the data are ranked to gap and no gap. Gap means there are nan in the files when the tracker can not identify the positions of the fish, in nogap the tracker created positions in function of the previous and next entries. For each file, the first column shows the time, then the columns go by two (x position and y position) and keep the identity of each fish.
